# ANIMO-Spear
Official Repository for De La Salle University - Dasmarinas Basic Education Department's Engineering and Robotics Club, ANIMO spear 

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for Animo Spear, the premier student organization for Engineering and Robotics at De La Salle University - Dasmariñas. We are a community of innovators, builders, and dreamers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and automation.🤖 

# About Us

Animo Spear serves as a hub for DLSU-D students to apply classroom theory to real-world engineering challenges. From competitive robotics to sustainable hardware solutions, we foster an environment of collaborative learning and technical excellence.

# 📂 Repository Structure

This organization houses a variety of projects, including: Robotics-Core: Source code for our competitive bots (Sumobot, Line Tracers, etc.).

IoT-Solutions: Projects focused on smart campus initiatives and environmental monitoring.
Workshop-Materials: Open-source resources, schematics, and tutorials from our technical training sessions.
Firmware-Library: Reusable drivers and modules for Arduino, ESP32, and Raspberry Pi platforms.

Our members work with a diverse set of tools and languages: C, C++, Python, GCL (LabVIEW), JavaHardwareArduino, STM32, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, PLCsDesign/CADAutodesk Fusion 360, SolidWorks, Eagle PCBSimulationMATLAB, Proteus, and TinkerCAD.
